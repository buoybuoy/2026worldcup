ℹ️ How are these rooting estimates calculated?

For each person, and each of today's games, we test all three results — home win, draw, away win — and pick the one that's best for their predictions:

  1. Project the rest of every group to completion, assuming the remaining games finish the way that person predicted.
  2. Score each projected result the same way as the leaderboard:
    • Group finish — 1st 25 · 2nd 15 · 3rd 10 · 4th 5
    • Third-place advance — +5 for each of their picks that lands in the top-8 third-place teams
  3. Whichever result scores highest is the column their name appears in.

• The number on a chip (e.g. "2nd") is where they predicted that team to finish.
• "/ draw ok" means a draw ties as a best result too.
• A green chip means their good result is happening right now.
